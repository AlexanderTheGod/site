About me

Alexander Karpenko
Hi my name is Alexander. Unfortunately or fortunately, this is not just a "lorem" text. I would like to tell you a little bit about myself, but it won't be a happy story at all. I hate almost everything around me. Most people do not cause me anything but aggression, I have to hide my real emotions behind a mask of a smile. Sometimes I don't even want to get up in the morning. I've already said a lot of bad things, but it's true. Despite all this, there is one very good feeling in me, it is Love, but unfortunately unrequited. Maybe that's why I hate everything around me... maybe.

And so... what about Love?

Not so long ago I understand what Love is. Why i write about it? i dont know, i just want. Anyway nobody wont notice it. I'm a romantic at heart, but I can't show it. It's probably hard to imagine that I really Love one girl, and that it hurts me a lot.
